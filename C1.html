Protein description

Mediates copper transfer across the plastid envelope.

  • Required for the delivery of copper into the plastid stroma, which is essential for the function of copper proteins.
  • Seems to be selective for monovalent copper Cu+ transport.
  • Also plays a role in glucose signaling-mediated cell proliferation of root meristem in non-green tissues.
