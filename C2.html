Protein description

GTPase involved in protein precursor import into chloroplasts.

  • Seems to recognize chloroplast-destined precursor proteins and regulate their presentation to the translocation channel through GTP hydrolysis.
  • Binds GTP, GDP, XTP, but not ATP.
  • Probably specialized in the import of nuclear encoded photosynthetic preproteins from the cytoplasm to the chloroplast, especially during early development stages.
