Welcome to TPL Database

In the TPL Database you will get

  • Basic information on transmembrane proteins from within human mitochondria:

    Protein profile, protein ID, protein length, protein active site, etc.

  • Basic information on transmembrane proteins and ligands from within human mitochondria:

    Ligand ID, ligand structure, basic parameters of transmembrane protein-ligand binding, etc.

  • Basic information on transmembrane proteins from chloroplasts of Arabidopsis thaliana：

    Protein profile, protein ID, protein length, gene name, etc.

  • Basic information on transmembrane proteins and ligands from chloroplasts of Arabidopsis thaliana：

    Ligand name, ligand structure, etc.
